My Journey

Born by The Sea

At Alexandria, Egypt my journey started

I was born in Alexandria, a historic city on the Mediterranean Sea in Egypt, known for its rich culture and stunning coastline.

Despite its grandeur, life felt limited due to Egypt's centralized system, pushing me to explore beyond my surroundings.

My passion for computers ignited in my teenage years, where I spent countless hours learning about hardware and software.

Accountant.. What!

Bringing new technology to the work field

I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting at Alexandria University, laying a strong foundation in financial management.

After graduating, I worked in large companies, where my Excel skills and data tools expertise earned me a promotion to Senior Accountant.

However, my ambition to explore new fields and challenges grew stronger, setting the stage for a career shift.

New life, New Career, welcome in Cairo

at the media carrer started the journy into the data

I moved to Cairo, a bustling metropolis of 35 million, to embark on a new career in media during a time of regional transformation.

After exploring various media roles, I found my niche in data journalism, uncovering insights from government and public data.

This experience honed my analytical skills and prepared me for impactful projects ahead.

Let's fight the fake news.. important stage of my life

With my dear collegues Fighting the fake news through the internet

In 2019, I founded FALSO, meaning "fake" in Egyptian slang, to combat misinformation as one of Egypt’s first fact-checking initiatives.

Leading a team of 60 volunteers, we tackled false news, gaining significant traction during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Our work reached a wide audience, earning national recognition and reinforcing my commitment to societal impact.

Oh Canada!

My amazing family for their happiness I will do every thing

In 2023, I made the life-changing decision to move my family to Canada, seeking love, opportunity, and a better future.

We arrived in mid-2024, and every day reinforces that this was the right choice, especially seeing my daughter Rima thrive.

She’s learning about diversity and making friends from around the world, filling our new home with joy.

Back to School.. What a journey

At Algonquin college Volunteering to organize an event

Recognizing the need to pivot careers in Canada, I enrolled in the Computer Programming diploma at Algonquin College in 2024.

Combining my finance and data background with programming, I’ve excelled with a 3.87 GPA in my first two terms.

This journey is driven by my love for my daughter Rima, aiming to provide her with the best opportunities possible.
